🌿 Welcome to Planteer!

At Planteer, we believe that plants are not just beautiful decorations, but living beings that add life to our homes and workplaces. Our goal is to create a comprehensive plant database, helping plant lovers easily find the information they need.

🌱 What do we offer?

🔍 Comprehensive Search – Easily search for any type of plant and get accurate information.
📚 Plant Care Guide – Tips and advice on how to best care for your plants.

💬 Comment System – Share your experiences and thoughts with the community of plant lovers.
